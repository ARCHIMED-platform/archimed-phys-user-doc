Render the output

After executing ARCHIMED in the example folder, the simulation outputs are found at this path: ./output/simdir. There are several files here, and one is especially useful for visualizing the results in 3D: coffee.opf.

To render the results, please download XPlo from the AMAPStudio website. Then open XPlo, press ctrl+I, choose opf, and navigate to the location of coffee.opf and open it.

Xplo will open and 3D render the example coffee. Now you can color the coffee components by any output variable by clicking on: Geometry -> Colorizer.
